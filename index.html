OpenClaw is an open-source AI agent framework that brings intelligent automation to your fingertips. It's designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing workflows ‚Äî a powerful assistant that lives in your chat apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack) and can automate repetitive tasks, answer questions, process data, and much more ‚Äî all without requiring you to write a single line of code.

First you purchase your package, and then we will reach out by email or call to know what parts of your business you want to integrate with the AI Agent. We will ask you the preferred way to chat with your AI Agent: WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack or any other messaging app. Each setup lives in an isolated environment and we can use privacy-first LLM models so you can rest assured that your data remains yours. Can I call my agent with voice?

Yes! Voice interaction is available for your AI agent. You can speak to your agent naturally, just like you would with a colleague. However, please note that voice features incur additional charges based on usage (minutes of voice processing). We'll provide transparent pricing details when setting up voice capabilities for your specific use case.

Can I install OpenClaw for free?

Yes, if you are a tinkerer and tech savvy you can go to the OpenClaw GitHub repository and install it, but you need to know about API keys, SDK configurations and there is no free support. You also need to host the AI Agent and we don't recommend installing it in your personal computer without sandboxing, due to it could expose your personal data, loss access to your email or YouTube accounts or even get fraudulent access to your business or self-custody hot wallets.
